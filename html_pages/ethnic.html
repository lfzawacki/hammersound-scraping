This is a thumb piano, an African instrument also known as a timbura or kalimba the note is make by plicking strips of steel over a piece of wood or (in my case) a sound box, the sound created is a woody xylophone like plucking sound. Actually if you play scalic music on it, it could be mistaken for a xylophone, so imagine your playing a thumb piano. All the recording gear used is good, RoDE NT3 Condencer Mic, 20 - 20 flat freq into SBLive.
