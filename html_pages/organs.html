This soundfonts is based on an old German Honer Accordian. Its in the key of E. A SHURE SM58 Mic was used to sample it at 44100 Hz 16 bit. The feel of the accordian has been captured closely by sampling the 3 separate parts - the Single notes, Chorus Notes that play multiple reeds, and the 4 Drones that are played by the left hand as a sort of a bass. The sound of it may not blow you off your feet like a good flute, piano or string instrument might, but when it comes to accordians I think this is near as good as it gets.
